## Core Principles of This Handbook

1. **Pattern over problems** вЂ” Learn 15 patterns, solve 150+ problems
2. **Trade-offs over answers** вЂ” Interviewers want your thinking, not a textbook answer
3. **Depth over breadth** вЂ” Know 5 things deeply > 50 things superficially
4. **Practice over reading** вЂ” Each section has actionable exercises
5. **Story over bullets** вЂ” Your experience should tell a narrative
